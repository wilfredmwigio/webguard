🚨 Reporting vs. Tattling

Do you know the difference? It matters a LOT online!

📣 Tattling = getting someone in trouble.
🛡️ Reporting = keeping someone safe!
  • If something online makes you feel scared, sad, or unsafe — REPORT it to a trusted adult.
  • Reporting is brave and smart, never mean.
  • You can always tell a parent, teacher, or older sibling.

🦁 Jambo says: "In my pride, we all look out for each other. Online, YOU can do the same!"

🔒 Is This Website Safe?

Always check your browser before entering ANY personal info!

🔴 "Not Secure" = DANGER — do NOT share passwords or personal info!
🟢 "Secure / 🔒 https://" = Safe to use
  • Always look for the padlock icon 🔒 and https:// in the address bar.
  • If it says "Not Secure" in red — close it and tell an adult.
  • Scammers create fake websites that look real — always double-check the web address.

🦁 "A wise lion checks the watering hole before drinking. Always check the padlock before you type!"

🚫 What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is using technology to hurt, embarrass, or threaten someone. Look at all the forms it takes:

❌ These are ALL cyberbullying:

Spreading rumors · Sending threats · Gossiping online · Posting insults · Name-calling · Harassment · Lies · Mean words · Teasing that hurts

🛡️ If you see or experience any of these online — Screenshot it, Block the person, and TELL a trusted adult immediately!

🦁 "Even one mean message online can cause real pain. Be kind — and be brave enough to report!"

📸 Screenshots = Digital Evidence!

When something bad happens online, your most powerful tool is capturing the evidence!

📱 How to take a screenshot:
• Android Phone: Power + Volume Down (hold 2 sec)
• iPhone: Side button + Volume Up
• Computer: Print Screen key or Snipping Tool (Win+Shift+S)
  • Screenshot mean messages, threats, or scary content before anything else.
  • Save it — do NOT delete it. It is legal evidence!
  • Show it to a trusted adult immediately.
  • Screenshots help parents, teachers, and police take action.

📋 The 3-Step Evidence Rule:

1️⃣ Capture — Take the screenshot now
2️⃣ Save — Keep it, don't delete it
3️⃣ Show — Tell a trusted adult today

🦁 "Evidence is powerful! Digital bullies leave tracks — just like lions in the savanna!"

🔍 The S.I.F.T. Method — Fact-Check Everything!

Before you share ANY news online, use S.I.F.T. to verify it!

✋ S — Stop
Pause before sharing emotional content.
🔎 I — Investigate
Check who wrote it — are they credible?
📰 F — Find Coverage
Is this story on trusted news sites too?
🗺️ T — Trace Claims
Find the original source — not a screenshot!
🦁 Jambo says: "Don't just consume — be a critical verifier! Real lions check danger before reacting!"
